New Shows and Movies Coming to and Leaving Netflix in November 2020

Enjoy scores of new movies and series starting November 1.

Eric Vega October 23, 2020
3 minute read
Advertiser Disclosure: At Slickdeals, we work hard to help you find the best deals. To do this, some of the products featured here are from our partners, who may provide us with compensation. However, this doesn’t influence our opinions. Our views are our own.

Spooky season is coming to an end, and Netflix is pivoting hard in November with a ton of new holiday content. There’s also a little bit of everything coming to the service in November, including hit movies and classic series. Cinema buffs will be happy to hear that films like Platoon, A Clockwork Orange and Fruitvale Station. If non-fiction TV is your preference, Netflix is adding new episodes of Jeopardy! and Survivor to the library as well.

New content also means the loss of existing titles, and November is no exception. Netflix will be saying goodbye to some popular titles, including the Ocean’s trilogy, Moneyball and Anaconda. Here are all of the changes coming to Netflix in November.

Leveling-up Your Streaming Devices

If you’re looking to upgrade your streaming setup while saving on a Netflix subscription, you might be interested in this new offer from Google.

You can get the latest Chromecast with Google TV and a 6-month standard Netflix subscription for $89.99. The latest Chromecast device retails at $49.99, while a 6-month Netflix subscription would cost about $77.94. That gives this offer a total value of $127.93, which means the $90 price tag is discounted at roughly 30%.
