PUMA Discount Tips

PUMA Discount Tips 

You don't have to pay full price on your favorite lifestyle and athleisure fashions for women, men and children. It's always a good idea to visit this page to see if there are any printable promo codes or online coupons currently available. Even if there are no promo codes that work on your order, you can still find a good deal with these savings tips and best practices.

How to Use a PUMA Coupon

The process of using PUMA coupon codes is pretty straightforward. If you're shopping online, navigate to the Shopping Bag page once you have finished browsing. Enter your coupon code or discount code in the field provided and hit "Redeem" to apply your discount. The amount taken off should be reflected on the Order Summary page. PUMA does not have a written policy on stackable coupons. Certain promos might stack on top of one another, but you shouldn’t always expect it. 

If you are shopping in-store, it's even easier. All you have to do to collect your in-store deal is to show your printable coupon to the sales associate at checkout. 

Save When You Shop the Sales 

You can find all of the latest deals and online discounts in the Sale sections on the PUMA website. When you click on the "Men", "Women" and "Kids" sections, you can select "Sale" and narrow your search based on size, price, color and other categories.

It’s always a good idea to visit your local PUMA store before completing an online order. There might be store-specific holiday sales and in-store promo codes that may not be reflected by their online deals page.  

Join the PUMA Email Program

When you sign up for an account on the PUMA website, you'll get special offers, free shipping opportunities and other exclusive perks. On top of that, sign up is completely free and only takes a minute. You can enter your information on the pop-up when you enter the website, or you can scroll to the bottom of the page and enter it in the provided field. From time to time, PUMA even offers a deal where you get 10% off your next purchase when you sign up.

PUMA does not offer a loyalty program or rewards program. If you want to get the best prices and stay in the loop on all things PUMA, joining the email list is your best bet. 

Status-Based Discounts 

If you're a student or a veteran, PUMA has a special discount for you. Students can enjoy a 10% off student discount when they verify their student status via UNiDAYS. Active and veteran military personnel also receive 10% off of each purchase. 

Doctors, nurses and first responders save even bigger, with a whopping 20% off discount. Military and service members must verify their status through ID.me. Unfortunately, it doesn’t look like PUMA offers a senior citizen discount at this time. 

PUMA Return Policy 

If you need to return your recent PUMA purchase, you can get a full refund as long as you provide proof of purchase and initiate your return within 45 days after the original purchase. Purchases made on the PUMA website qualify for a free return, which you can start on your "My Account" page. In addition to in-store purchases, you can also return items bought online at your local PUMA shop.

PUMA Price Adjustment Policy 

If your recent PUMA purchase goes on sale or gets a price reduction within 14 days of your original purchase, you are allowed to request a one-time price adjustment. You can't do this if you used a promo code or coupon code on your original purchase. To request a price adjustment, contact PUMA's customer service team. Speak to them about price matching their current sale offer and PUMA may provide a cashback option.

Purchase $35 worth of your favorite PUMA collections and you'll automatically score free standard shipping. This shipping deal includes orders shipped to all 50 states.

Contact PUMA Customer Service

If you have any issues with your recent credit card purchase, gift card or price adjustment request, this retailer offers a few different ways to get in touch with their customer service team. You can reach their customer service phone number by dialing 1 (888) 565-PUMA. For live chat and email support, visit their Contact Us page for more information.
