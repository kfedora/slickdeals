About B&H Photo Video

B&H Photo Video started in 1973 as a "mom and pop" shop with one employee. Thanks to its dedication to honest sales and quality stock, the store rapidly gained a following of loyal photo, video and audio professionals. Today, B&H is one of the largest non-chain specialty electronics sellers in the U.S., with over 3 million enthusiasts and professionals relying on it for their tech gear. B&H employees spend over 25% of their working time training on equipment to ensure they're experts in the products they sell. It goes to show that you don’t have to sacrifice quality for affordability — there are plenty of discount codes, online coupons and printable promo codes that can help you save even more on your photo and video equipment purchases.

B&H Photo Video Discount Tips

B&H offers exceptional prices every day on its extensive range of photography equipment, audio devices, mobile phones and entertainment products. Whether you're looking for brand-new or refurbished items, shop at B&H to get fantastic deals on all your cameras, video recorders and electronics. Check back here often to find new and exciting B&H Photo Video coupon codes and in-store promo codes to save on your next purchase. 

Get the Best Shipping Price at B&H Photo Video

Most products at B&H ship free, but when you spend over $49, your order automatically qualifies for free expedited shipping to addresses within the contiguous U.S., no coupon code necessary. Expedited shipping arrives within one to three business days and standard shipping within three to seven business days. 

Score B&H Photo Video Deals at the DealZone

Check out the B&H Photo Video Deal Zone section for daily promos. Specials only last for 24 hours, so get in quick before items sell out. Discounts start at $10 off so you always get a great bargain. Don’t forget to sign up for their email program so you never miss out on discounts, in-store coupons and other promotional material. This is especially handy during the holiday season, when Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals are in full effect. B&H gift cards can be used and purchased on the site, over the phone or in the superstore. Unfortunately, B&H Photo Video does not allow stackable coupons. 

 

Sign Up for My B&H Rewards 

If you are a regular customer at B&H, you can save by joining their loyalty program and signing up for a My B&H Rewards Card. You can earn points for every eligible purchase, which can be turned into a handy online promo code which you can use to apply online discounts at checkout. If you're shopping in-store at the B&H Superstore, all you have to do is show the cashier printed proof of membership, which you can bring along with your printable coupons. Your in-store deals kick in once your rewards card number is entered.

Sign Up for the B&H Photo Video Payboo Credit Card  

If you are making a large purchase or shop at B&H often, you should consider signing up for their credit card. There is no annual fee, and it offers a revolving line of credit that waives the price of sales tax, giving you a little cash back every time you use the card.

Refurbished Stock

If you're looking for an exceptional B&H Photo Video deal, check out the site's Used Department, where you could save hundreds or even thousands of dollars. There's a huge selection of secondhand electronics from cameras and accessories to televisions and computers. Save on a sound system or smartphone with the knowledge that used stock has been tested to ensure you get reliable, quality goods. Every item also comes with a 90-day warranty on parts and labor.

You Can Save Based On Your Status 

While everyone can take advantage of B&H's everyday online deals and discount codes, certain demographics can save a little extra. Students receive exclusive pricing, online and in-store discounts and free shipping when they verify their student discount status through UNiDAYS. It doesn't look like they have a senior citizen discount or military discount at this time. 

B&H Photo Video Return Policy 

If you have any issues with your purchase, you have 30 days to submit a return claim on the B&H Photo Video website. You can't return opened TVs and monitors, computers, software and other special-order items. Your refund will be credited towards the original form of payment.

B&H Photo Video Price Adjustment Policy 

B&H offers price matching and adjustment on a case by case basis, as used items, rentals and other factors make determining the true market value of an item difficult. Contact customer service for more information about whether or not your item is eligible for a price adjustment.
