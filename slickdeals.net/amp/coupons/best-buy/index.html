Best Buy Discount Tips

The Deals of the Day page is the place to go to find products at major discounts. It features a headline deal of the day among other online deals. You can frequently find deep online discounts on electronics and appliances. The offers change regularly, so it pays to take a quick look when you're in the market for a product that Best Buy carries.

It is always a good idea to visit your local Best Buy before completing an online purchase, as there may be store-specific in-store discounts, promos and clearance items that might not be reflected on the website.   

Find Best Buy Coupons

The Best Buy $10 off coupon code is very popular and will arrive from time to time via email if you’re signed up for their email program, which you can easily join on the bottom of the Best Buy web page. They also have printable coupons from time to time, but you don’t need to actually print them out in order to use them, you can just pull up the in-store coupon on your phone. If you’re moving, look for the 10% off Best Buy mover’s coupon that you can get from the post office. 

Best Buy allows for stackable coupons, so it’s worth trying out different combinations before checking out online. 

Pay with Visa Checkout

Best Buy partners with VISA several times a year (usually around the holiday season) for exclusive VISA Checkout offers, which have historically included $25 off your purchase if you make your purchase using VISA Checkout. 

Get the Best Shipping Price

If you buy more than $35 of merchandise, your Best Buy order will ship for free. This free shipping Best Buy offer automatically applies once your cart hits $35, not including taxes and coupon deductions. 

Sign Up for the My Best Buy Credit Card  

If you spend a lot of money at Best Buy or wish to finance a larger purchase, you can save by signing up for their credit card. You can get 5% back or more on your rewards-eligible purchases, flexible financing options and even 10% back on your first purchase using the card.

Take Advantage of Best Buy's Price Match Guarantee

Thanks to their price adjustment policy, Best Buy matches the advertised price of qualifying products from select online and local competitors. Simply provide documentation of the lower price to a store associate, via telephone or using the online chat. Best Buy will even price match its own sales during the 15-day return and exchange period.

Best Buy Return Policy

The standard return policy gives you 15 days to make a return after the purchase date. My Best Buy Elite and Elite Plus members have 30 and 45 days, respectively. Certain classes of items and products that are part of a specific program may have their own specific terms for returns. Check online before trying to make a return.

Save With Status Based Discounts 

Best Buy offers special savings for students on select merchandise. Verify your student status when you sign up for the Student Deals program and you'll receive emails with student-oriented Best Buy online coupons and printable promo codes. They always have great Back to School offers in late summer, such as $150 off select Apple computers or tablets. You can also get discounted software, tablets and gaming accessories if you’re a student. 

Members of the military can save 15% on their Best Buy online purchase. Some local stores offer a 10% discount while others don't offer it at all, so make sure to check before making your purchase. 

Best Buy does not currently offer a senior citizen discount. 

Join the My Best Buy Rewards Program

Get special Best Buy offers specifically tailored to your interests. Rack up rewards points with every purchase in stores or online, and use your points to pay for or reduce the cost of other purchases thanks to their cash back and percent-off kickbacks. You can also earn extra points by writing a product review for the website. If you spend enough to qualify for Elite or Elite Plus loyalty program tier, you can get more free shipping options, a longer return window and higher reward points percentages.

Contact Best Buy Customer Service 

If you have any questions about warranties, returns, your order or anything else, you can call the Best Buy customer service phone number at 1 (888) 237-8289. For tech support, live chat, FAQs and other inquiries, visit the Best Buy Contact Us page for more information.

More About Best Buy Coupons

What is Best Buy's price matching & free shipping policy?

Best Buy guarantees to price match any item sold by eligible competitors on-line and in-store. The items must be identical and be readily available for purchase. Exclusions for their price match policy will be reviewed by a store representative either in-store or on the phone. Free Shipping is offered on all purchases $35 and up as well as free next day shipping for qualifying purchases (based on availablility and size restrictions).

How do I get 10% off at Best Buy?

You can get 10% at Best Buy when you apply and get approved for their My BestBuy Credit Card. The 10% is applicable as cashback on your first purchse using the card.

Does Best Buy offer military, senior, or student discounts?

Best Buy does not have official discount policies for military and seniors but they do offer deals and discounts for students. You can access these deals by creating a bestbuy account and signing up for their Student Hub.

Do Best Buy coupons work on Apple?

Best Buy usually offers deals on Apple devices sold through them, but these are promotions specific to purchasing through Best Buy as a retailer. These coupons do not work when purchasing directly from Apple.
