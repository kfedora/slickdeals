Which Deals Should You Focus on at Harbor Freight This Black Friday?

Harbor Freight is already known for being relatively cheap compared to its competitors, so Black Friday can be a real haul if you play your cards right. You can walk into the store with just $20 and come out with a variety of handy tools. The lesson? Don't ignore the cheaper stuff. For example, you can get a Drill Master 4 1/2" Angle Grinder for $9.99 or a Voyager 12" Tool Bag for as little as $3.99.

If you're looking for some more heavy duty equipment, there are a few items sporting discounts in the $100+ range. Harbor Freight is selling their Predator 4000W and Predator 9000W generators for $299.99 and $589.99, respectively. There are also some big savings to be found on power tools, like the Hercules 10" 15AMP Jobsite Table Saw. Originally priced at $379.99, it will be available for just $249.99 on Black Friday.

What Time Does Harbor Freight Open on Black Friday?

  • Open from 7 a.m. to 9 p.m. on Friday Nov. 29th, and 8 a.m. to 9 p.m. on Saturday Nov. 30th.
  • Harbor Freight has not announced when their online sale will begin, but it will likely kick off on Black Friday. 
  • The store will be not open on Thanksgiving Day.

While you should always contact your local Harbor Freight to get precise info on their Black Friday schedule, the ad scan officially announced their extended Black Friday hours for November 29th and 30th. Store hours will return to normal on Sunday, December 1st.

When Is the Best Time to Shop?

  • Around lunchtime on Black Friday at physical locations
  • As soon as possible for online offers.

Harbor Freight is opening early on Black Friday, but that doesn't necessarily mean you have to be there at the crack of dawn. If you're looking to browse and maybe score on a few items without a lot of hassle, then you should probably do your shopping around lunchtime. There's typically a brief break in crowd size as people head to lunch, so you can shop the remaining merchandise in peace.

Of course, if your eyes are set on some of the more exciting discounts, then you'll want to hop on the sale as early as possible. Online will be your best bet to guarantee you get those big savings, but you'll have to act fast before they're all snatched up. Additionally, make sure the flat rate shipping fee of $6.99 on most orders doesn't eat up any savings you may have gotten if you shopped in the store.
