About Nordstrom

In 1901, Nordstrom was established and is now one of the top retailers in the fashion industry. It sells women’s, children’s and men’s clothing and other apparel. You can also find home decor and beauty products at Nordstrom. Buy branded goods and accessories from many high-end designers at affordable prices.

Nordstrom Coupon And Discount Tips 

The company's expansive Clearance section is where you’ll find the company’s items on sale at huge in-store discounts, sometimes over 50% off. Last-minute deals, anniversary sale specials, holiday specials, this is where you’ll find them--there’s nearly 9,000 items available on clearance right now. Everything from jeans to shoes to button-up shirts, home goods and so much more, practically every category of Nordstrom goods are available on clearance with a nearly endless variety of choices. No Nordstrom coupon is necessary either, these clearance sales are for everyone.

These are all automatic promos, so you don’t have to enter a coupon code or discount code or anything like that to get these sale prices. The store doesn’t offer stackable coupons per se, but with significantly slashed prices and the ability to earn cash back on your purchases with a Nordstrom card, there’s a ton of room to save money without an in-store promo code.

Nordstrom’s Referral program is another way to save without needing a discount code. Here’s how it works: when you refer a friend to Nordstrom using their dedicated referral link, your friend will receive a $50 credit for their first purchase. As soon as their first order goes through, you’ll also receive a $50 credit to your account. That’s $100 in value, a free gift just waiting to be opened. No Nordstrom promo code needed.

As of August 2020, Nordstrom doesn’t offer a dedicated student discount, military discount or senior citizen discount. As the company’s coupon codes are primarily for the online store, there are no printable coupons or printable coupon codes. 

Nordstrom Email Program

Nordstrom lacks a dedicated email program, but it does offer its Nordstrom smartphone apps for customers to receive online promo codes for product discounts. For instance, your first $150 purchase using the Nordstrom app will earn you $20 off, no promo code needed. Download the Nordstrom app to see which online coupons you can benefit from.

Nordstrom Rewards Program, Credit Card And Credit Card

The company offers two credit cards and one loyalty program: the store-specific Nordstrom store card, and the Nordstrom Visa credit card, and the Nordy Club membership club. Both credit cards offer cash back for standard purchases, while the Visa credit card also offers reduced cash back benefits on all card purchases, not just Nordstrom or Nordstrom Rack. Earn 3 points per dollar at Nordstrom and Nordstrom Rack when you shop with the credit card, and you can earn 1-2 points back per dollar on travel, grocery and other non-Nordstrom purchases with the Visa card. Nordy Club exclusive members earn 1 point back per dollar spent. 

Both card programs offer fantastic online discounts, including a $40 Nordstrom Note just for signing up. For a full breakdown of the specific online deals each card offers, see the full card comparison here.

For a dedicated gift card option, head to the store's customizable Gift Card section. Nordstrom gift cards are available as physical cards and as e-gifts. For a friend or loved one, there's nothing quite like getting a free gift from Nordstrom.

Nordstrom Return Policy And Price Adjustment Policy 

Unused and undamaged products can be returned for a full and free refund within 30 days of purchase, with original receipt attached. Returns are typically processed within 10-14 days if they qualify for a full refund. You can check the full return policy guidelines here. Nordstrom offers products at a deep markdown that have gone through a price matching algorithm: you can shop their price-matched items here, or request a price adjustment if you see an identical item offered elsewhere for less by clicking here.

Get the Best Shipping Price

Nordstrom offers 100% free shipping and free returns on all of its product orders--even to Alaska and Hawaii, which many of its competitors can’t boast. No minimum purchase and no in-store promo code needed. Simply place your order, check out, and the price you see is the price you’ll pay. Doesn’t get much better than standard free shipping.
