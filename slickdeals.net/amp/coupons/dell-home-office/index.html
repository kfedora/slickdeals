Dell Home & Office Discount Tips

As one of the first and best known computer technology companies on the market, Dell has become a household name thanks to the company's vast and varied selection of PCs, tablets, gaming hardware and other electronic essentials. It doesn't matter if you are looking for a simple, serviceable laptop or outfitting your entire office with new PCs—Dell Home and Office has got you covered for all of your hardware, software, workstation and storage needs. When you know how to spot a good deal at Dell Home and Office, you don’t need to break the bank to afford some of the finest new electronics on the market.

Dell Home and Office Discount Tips 

Paying for a new PC or home office setup can be a costly endeavor up front, but it’s worth making a sound investment that can be enjoyed for years to come. Fortunately, you might not even have to pay that much—Dell Home and Office provides more than a few ways for its customers to save on cloud storage, 4K televisions and other electronics. Dell has long been known for their budget-friendly computers that offer utilitarian performance and simplistic design. Lucky for us, they’ve kept with the low-cost theme with laptops like the XPS 15 starts at $999 — but added superior performance and premium design elements. Be sure to visit this page often so you can stay informed on all of the latest deals and discounts available at Dell Home and Office. 

Finding Dell Home and Office Coupons and Deals 

In addition to all of the offers and promotions here on the Dell Home and Office store page, you can also view a current list of promotions, online promo codes and price reductions on the Coupons and Coupon Codes page on the Dell website. If you check this page often, you can probably find the latest coupon code for anything from gaming peripherals to your home office setup. If you want a comprehensive view of Dell's current range of products and their current promotions, it's worth checking out the Dell Catalog. This weekly ad-style circular is one of the best places to find out about upcoming sales events, releases and flash sales.

Dell does not have a typical procedure regarding stackable coupons. Some online promo codes can be combined with other offers such as free shipping and Dell Rewards credit, while others are limited to one per transaction. Since Dell does not offer printable promo codes, you can add the promo code right before you check out online. Check the terms and conditions of your discount code before completing your order. 

Subscribe to the Dell Home and Office Email Program

Do you want to be among the first to hear about upcoming product releases, exclusive online coupons and other exciting technology updates from Dell Home and Office? It only takes a moment to sign up for the company's email newsletter. Having your finger on the pulse of the latest Dell news could really pay off during the holiday shopping season, since you'll be first in line for flash deals and other exciting limited time offers on Black Friday and Cyber Monday.

Join the Dell Home and Office Rewards Program 

Spend a lot of money at Dell Home and Office? You can save on your purchases and even earn money towards future ones by joining Dell Rewards, the Dell Home and Office loyalty program. Dell Rewards is free to join and you can start earning between 3% and 6% in rewards credit on eligible purchases. Once you've racked up a certain dollar amount in rewards, you can use them to pay for laptops, gaming PCs, televisions and more.

Finance Your Dell Home and Office Purchase 

Is there a certain Dell gaming laptop or server calling out to you, but you aren't sure if you can afford to pay for it up front? You can finance your Dell Home and Office purchase by financing it with a Dell Preferred Account. This revolving line of credit requires a pre-qualification and application, but you don't need to use a credit card to take advantage of this online deal. You can even save on select products when you choose to pay using your Dell Preferred Account, and it doesn't hurt that account holders also enjoy exclusive promotional offers, cash back opportunities and up to 6% back in Dell Rewards.

Status-Based Discounts 

Everyone has access to the same coupon codes and online discounts, but some groups can save a little extra on their next Dell purchase. If you are currently enrolled in school, the Dell University program offers a selection of student discounts on laptops, tablets and other school essentials. Students aren't the only ones who can take advantage of these status-based promos. If you are an active or retired member of the U.S. military and you can verify your status with a military email address, Dell will give you a 10% military discount on eligible purchases. Retirees are also able to save 10% on PCs and other electronics, thanks to Dell's senior citizen discount. 

Dell Home and Office Return Policy 

Most Dell products can be returned for a full refund within 30 days of the date on your packing slip or invoice. You must obtain a Credit Return Authorization from Dell before shipping your return back to the company, and you may have to pay a restocking fee and return shipping fees depending on the circumstances of your return. Software, gift cards, non-Dell branded mobile phones and most Dell Technologies services are non-returnable items. Note that Dell items purchased from third-party retailers cannot be returned through the Dell website, so you must adhere to whichever store-specific return policies apply for other vendors. 

Dell Home and Office Price Adjustment Policy 

Thanks to Dell's Price Match Guarantee, you can rest assured knowing that you will always pay the lowest possible price on all of your technology needs. If you find a lower price on an identical or comparable product sold by Dell or one of its competitors, you can request a price match. In the event that you find a lower price on a Dell product that you have already purchased, you can request a price adjustment within 30 days of your invoice date.

Get the Best Shipping Price

While you might be spending a precious penny or two on a new computer, you don't have to worry about the cost of shipping. Any purchase on the Dell Home and Office website automatically qualifies for free shipping, and you don't even have to hit a spending minimum or apply a Dell free shipping promo code. If you are a Dell Rewards member, you might be able to score free expedited delivery on certain products.

Contact Dell Home and Office Customer Service

Do you have questions about technical issues, order support or any other related inquiries? Dell's customer service team is here to help. The Dell Home and Office customer service phone number is 1 (800) 624-9897. If you would prefer to speak with a representative over live chat or email, you can find more information on the Dell Home and Office Contact Us page.
