J.Crew Discount Tips

Discover great pieces that withstand the test of time without breaking the bank, thanks to all of the deals that J.Crew has to offer. Visit this page before you begin shopping, so you don’t miss out on any valuable money-saving J.Crew coupons. Before you complete an order on the website, it might be worth visiting your local brick-and-mortar store. You never know what store-specific promotions might be running, plus you get to use an in-store J.Crew coupon code. Check out these discount tips that will help you save on your favorite styles.

Finding and Using a J.Crew Promo Code 

In addition to all of the deals and online discounts here on the J.Crew store page, you can also view all of the current promo codes and special savings events right on the J.Crew website. Many coupon codes are listed at the on the top of the webpage, and you can view a full list of discounts by clicking the "See All Offers" link in the top right hand corner of the page. Once you've found the right coupon for your order, you can apply it by typing the code into the "Enter promo code" field below your subtotal. If you're shopping in-store, it's even easier. All you have to do is show your printable promo code or in-store coupon to the cashier, who will apply it to your order.

J.Crew does not allow the use of stackable coupons. Choose your coupon code or in-store promo code carefully before completing your order. 

Subscribe to the J.Crew Email Program

Want to be one of the first to hear about new product releases, style news, in-store deals and printable coupons? It only takes a minute to sign up for the J.Crew email newsletter. When you provide your birthday, the company will even hook you up with a special coupon code or store credit on your special day. If you already have an account on the site, you can also join the email list by enabling email updates.

Join the J.Crew Rewards Program 

Shop and save by earning rewards from J.Crew through the J.Crew Rewards program. You can earn 1 point for every $1 spent, and when you reach 200 points the store will give you a $5 reward. In addition, rewards members get free shipping, early access to holiday sales events and promos as well as exclusive J.Crew discount codes and online coupons. Signing up is free and easy. All you have to do is enter your email address on their website and you are automatically enrolled in the program. 

J.Crew Credit Card and Cash Back

Want to take your J.Crew Rewards membership to the next level? You should consider applying for the J.Crew Credit Card. Cardholders receive twice as many points per $1 spent compared with a regular loyalty program member. On top of the amenities of a J.Crew Rewards membership, the J.Crew Credit Card also comes with access to an exclusive customer service number and free alterations on any items purchased with your card.

Students and Teachers Save With Status-Based Discounts 

Teachers and college students are eligible to receive a 15% off educator and student discount on their J.Crew purchase. To claim this online deal, verify your status with SheerID on the J.Crew website. Once you've been verified, you will receive a unique online coupon that you can use on your next order. If you want to get some in-store discounts, show your student or teacher ID to a store associate and the deal will be applied to your order, you don't even have to use an in-store coupon. 

It doesn't look like J.Crew is currently offering a military discount or a senior citizen discount.

J.Crew Return Policy 

Is your J.Crew order not quite what you expected? You can get a full refund or exchange on your purchase if you initiate the return process within 30 days of your original purchase. The item(s) must be in unwashed, unworn condition, and you can't return lingerie, final sale items or anything that has been altered or customized. You can print a prepaid return label, which will automatically deduct $7.50 from your refund. When your return is processed, your refund will be returned to the original method of payment.

J.Crew Price Adjustment Policy 

In the event that an item you recently purchased goes on sale right after you bought it, you're not totally out of luck. J.Crew will honor a price adjustment on the item as long as you request it within seven days of the original purchase or ship date. You can't request a price adjustment on something that was already marked down when you bought it.

Get the Best Shipping Price

Joining J.Crew Rewards is the easiest way to score free shipping on qualifying orders, and you don’t even have to use an online promo code to enjoy this deal. The only real caveat is that you can’t get free shipping on the purchase of gift cards or on past orders. Otherwise, you will most likely have to pay the $5 flat rate shipping fee.

Contact J.Crew Customer Service

Questions, comments and concerns about orders, returns and other inquiries can be answered through a couple of different channels. The J.Crew customer service phone number is 1 (800) 562-0258. You can also speak with a representative via live chat, Twitter or by sending an email, you can find more information on J.Crew's Contact Us page.
