About BJs Wholesale

BJ's Wholesale Club first introduced the warehouse club concept to the northeastern United States in 1984. In the years to follow, this mega-retailer became a major hub for affordable groceries, home appliances, decor and thousands of other discounted selections. In order to be able to provide such low prices, the BJ’s Wholesale business model relies on a club membership, which is not expensive and pays for itself if you shop at this retailer often. BJ's is dedicated to providing members with prices significantly lower than those found in supermarkets, supercenters, department stores, drug stores and specialty retail stores. If BJ’s everyday in-store discounts and online deals are not discounted enough for your liking, you’re in luck. On top of the deals and benefits of a BJ’s club membership, you can cut costs even more by keeping track of the latest printable promo codes and online coupons that this retailer has to offer. Even beyond the scope of what BJ’s sells in stores, you can score some attractive promotional pricing on tires, glasses and even your cell phone bill. BJs Wholesale Discount Tips

BJ's Wholesale Club Discount Tips 

Not quite sure about this club membership thing? When one is available, you should take advantage of frequent offers for a free three-month membership. Memberships start at $55 a year and offer tons of perks, like 25% off grocery store prices and exclusive member services, such as BJ’s travel packages and BJ’s tires center deals. Once you’re in, you can start collecting online promo codes and in-store coupons to help you save even more on your household necessities. Even when there aren’t any coupon codes that apply to your order, you can still ensure that you’re getting the best deal by signing up for the BJ’s Perks Mastercard, subscribing to the company’s email newsletter and qualifying for free shipping whenever possible. 

Finding and Using a BJ’s Coupon Code 

In addition to what you see here on the BJ's store page, you can also view a current list of promo codes and online discounts on the My Coupons page or by downloading the BJ's app. The process of collecting these in-store coupons and online discount codes couldn't be easier, as you can simply click "Clip" to add the discount to your cart. Naturally, this means that BJ's not only allows but encourages stackable coupons. However, you cannot combine coupons of the same type in the same order. You can apply a paper coupon, a digital coupon and a certain number of clipless coupons to your order. 

Before completing a purchase on the BJ’s website, it’s worth visiting your local retail store beforehand. You never know if there might be store-specific clearance deals and promos, plus you get to use any printable coupons you have collected.

Subscribe to the BJ's Wholesale Club Email Program

Want to hear about the latest product releases, in-store deals, cash back opportunities and other money-saving information from BJ's Wholesale? It only takes a moment to sign up for the company's newsletter. To subscribe, scroll to the bottom of the webpage and enter your information in the form provided. If you already have an account on the BJ's site, you can also update your email preferences under your account settings.

Since BJ’s Wholesale effectively operates as one giant rewards program, the company does not offer a points-based loyalty program. Subscribing to the email list is the best way to stay in touch with the company and make sure that you don’t miss out on any exciting offers. 

Sign Up for the BJ’s Wholesale Credit Card 

Do you shop at BJ's often or spend a lot of money when you do? You might benefit from applying for the BJ's Perks Mastercard. If approved, you can earn up to 5% in cash back on your BJ's purchases, save 10 cents per gallon of gas at BJ's gas stations, 2% cash back while dining out and 1% cash back anywhere else that Mastercard is accepted. As you accrue rewards, you can spend them in $20 increments in-store or on the BJ's website. To make things convenient, your BJ's Perks Mastercard doubles as your club membership card.

Save With Status-Based Discounts 

While everyone gets to enjoy the same low prices and exclusive offers at BJ's, some groups can save a little extra thanks to the company's status-based discounts. If you are an active or retired member of the U.S. military, you can claim a 25% off military discount on your yearly membership plus a $10 coupon to spend as you'd like. Students can save money on a club membership as well. If you're currently enrolled in school, you can verify your student status with UNiDAYS and claim a student discount that takes $30 off of your Inner Circle membership.

BJ's Wholesale Club Return Policy and Price Adjustment Policy 

BJ's return policy depends on the category of item(s) that you wish to return. Generally speaking, merchandise must be in new condition and with accessories and packaging intact. Depending on the item and the reason for its return, you have anywhere from 14 days to a full year to request a refund. For a comprehensive list of each category’s return guidelines, read the BJ’s Wholesale Return Policy. Gift cards, final sale items, opened CDs and software and items that fall under a manufacturer's warranty cannot be returned. Unless the return is due to company error or a defective product, you are still responsible for the cost of return shipping. 

BJ’s Wholesale does not provide price adjustments. 

Get the Best Shipping Price

The cost of shipping largely depends on what you have bought, where you live and whether or not the item is part of a free shipping promotion. Many products, especially appliances and large electronics, always come with complimentary free shipping. Some categories may have a minimum spending threshold—for instance, sometimes you can enjoy free shipping if you spend over $35 on groceries. Otherwise, the cost of shipping is calculated based on your location. If you want to avoid shipping altogether, opt for the Free Pick Up Today option.

Contact BJ's Wholesale Club Customer Service

Have questions about a BJ’s purchase, return or a related inquiry? There are a few different ways to get in touch with the company’s customer support team. You can reach a representative by calling the customer service phone number at 1 (800) 257-2582. If you would prefer to submit a help ticket and correspond over email instead, you can do so on the BJ’s Wholesale Contact Us page.
