Microsoft Store Discount Tips

Whether you're looking for the latest software deals for setting up a new computer system or you just want a hot new game to pass the time, you'll find plenty of ways to save with Microsoft. From coupon codes to free shipping and other online discounts, there are a variety of ways to save big.

Microsoft Store Deals

Find deals on everything from computers to laptops and gaming consoles with Microsoft Store offers. Shop Xbox One deals and economize even more on gifts for others or yourself using printable promo codes and online coupons. You'll find discounts on accessories for consoles, tablets and mobile phones. Look for markdowns that make it easy to update your workspace, connect with family and play your favorite Microsoft Store Windows games. Check back often as the coupon code selection and available promos change frequently.

Unfortunately, the Microsoft Store does not allow for stackable coupons. Choose your coupon wisely before entering your promo code. 

Save With Free Shipping and In-Store Pickup

A Microsoft Store sale doesn't stop with your purchases. Take advantage of free shipping offers around the holidays or special events with no minimum purchase required. If you can't wait to enjoy your new computer software, skip the shipping price entirely and opt to pick up your items in-store for free.

Join the Microsoft Store Email Program 

Stay in the loop with the latest Microsoft Store news and be the first to hear about special promotions, in-store promo codes and special Microsoft Store events. This is especially useful during the holiday season, when Black Friday and Cyber Monday doorbuster deals come and go quickly. Don’t miss out on some of the best in-store discounts of the year! 

Sign Up for the Microsoft Store Rewards Program

Join Microsoft Rewards and you'll earn points to use towards gift cards, apps, games, sweepstakes entries and more. All you have to do is sign up with your email address. This loyalty program uses a unique play-based points system similar to that of a cash back bonus system, where you can earn points by using the search engine, playing games, completing daily offers and taking quizzes. You can win store gift cards, Xbox materials and other exclusive content. 

Microsoft Store Return Policy

If you want to make a return to the Microsoft Store, you will need to request a return from the company directly. If your product is eligible for a return, a Microsoft Store associate will help process your refund of exchange. The refund will be issued to your credit card or other original method of payment.

You Can Get Discounts Based On Your Status

Microsoft offers special pricing on computers, software, Xbox consoles and more products to certain demographics. There is a student discount, educator discount and military discount. Unfortunately, there is no senior citizen discount at this time.

Microsoft Store Price Adjustment Policy

The Microsoft Store will not price match the advertised prices from competitors. However, they will price adjust their own advertised prices within 30 days of your original purchase.

Microsoft Store Contact Info

If you have any questions about your order, a return or anything else, you can contact the Microsoft Store customer support at phone number at 1-877-696-7786. For in-person help, visit a local Answer Desk for assistance from experts on all your services and products. For any other inquiries, you can visit the Microsoft Store Contact Us page.
