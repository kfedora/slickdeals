About Dick's Sporting Goods

Dick's Sporting Goods is the premiere retailer for sporting goods, equipment and apparel. This one-time bait and tackle shop in upstate New York has turned into a sporting goods powerhouse. Along with its Golf Galaxy and Field & Stream subsidiaries, Dick's Sporting Goods has over 800 locations across the nation. We have compiled some of the best ways to help you save on sporting gear.

Dick's Sporting Goods Discount Tips

Use these online coupons, in-store discounts and printable promo codes, you can save big on your next trip to Dick's Sporting Goods.

How to Save at Dick’s Sporting Goods 

The easiest way to find out about the best in-store deals and discounts is by visiting "This Week's Deals" on the Dick's Sporting Goods website. You can find online discounts and promos separated into various categories. Taking advantage of these online deals in tandem with online promo codes is where you will really get the bang for your buck, especially around the holiday shopping season. It’s also worth visiting your local Dick’s Sporting Goods to check for store-specific deals such as clearance events and other sales you might not find online. 

It does not appear that Dick’s Sporting Goods offers a student discount or a senior citizen discount. Some locations will give a military discount of 10%, but it’s best to ask ahead of time if your store offers this discount. 

Dick’s Sporting Goods Coupon Code Tips 

Now that you know how to save on everyday purchases, it’s time to take advantage of Dick’s Sporting Goods coupon codes. Dick's Sporting Goods does not explicitly allow stackable coupons, but there are ways to use your printable coupons and in-store promo codes along with your rewards points to get a good deal.

Get the Best Shipping Price at Dick’s Sporting Goods 

While there is no set minimum for free shipping, Dick's Sporting Goods regularly has discount codes and online promotions for free shipping. Sometimes, it can be as cheap as $25 or more, while other times you may need to spend at least $65 to get free shipping. It’s always worth checking for a free shipping promo code before completing your order.

Join the ScoreCard Rewards Program 

When you join the free ScoreCard loyalty program, you earn one point for each dollar spent on an eligible purchase at Dick's Sporting Goods, Golf Galaxy and Field & Stream. You will receive a $10 reward for every 300 points earned, as well as exclusive offers available only to ScoreCard members. If you spend over $500 in a year, you will be upgraded to the ScoreCard Gold tier, which comes with even more benefits and rewards.

You can take your ScoreCard experience to the next level and sign up for the ScoreRewards Credit Card. With this card, you can earn double points on eligible purchases. You also get a $40 bonus reward for signing up, as well as access to special financing options

Sign Up for the Dick’s Sporting Goods Email Program and Text Alerts 

If you want to be the first to know about special promotions and offers at Dick’s Sporting Goods, you can sign up for their email program as well as their text alert program. An email sign-up will earn you 10% off your next purchase. When you text KICKOFF to 24001, you can get a $20 off of $100 purchase coupon that you can use as an in-store coupon or online. 

Take Advantage of Dick’s Sporting Goods Best Price Guarantee 

Dick's Sporting Goods has a lenient price matching and price adjustment policy. If you find a lower price on an identical product at a competing store, they will match the lower price so long as it is not excluded from a price match. You can price match in-store or online.

If you bought an item within the last 30 days and found it at a lower price, Dick's Sporting Goods will give you a price adjustment with proof of purchase. Even if it’s not much, a little extra cash back in your pocket never hurts. If you don't bring your receipt, you will most likely get a smaller amount on a store credit card.

Dick’s Sporting Goods Return Policy 

You have 60 days to return your purchase to Dick's Sporting Goods. Returns with original sales receipts will be refunded by the original form of payment, while gift cards and gift receipts will be repaid through a store credit card. Not all items are eligible for return, so make sure you can return your item before bringing it into the store or mailing it.

Contact Dick’s Sporting Goods Customer Service 

If you have any questions, comments or concerns, you can contact Dick’s Sporting Goods customer service phone number at 1 (877) 846-9997. For live chat, FAQs and other problem solving options, you can visit their Contact Us page for more information.
