Samsung Discount Tips

You can save a bundle on electronics when you shop with Samsung promo codes. For more savings opportunities, look at these useful tips.

Free Shipping and Returns  

The store will treat you to free shipping so you’ll have more money to spend on your latest tech acquisition. It also pays for return shipping on most products.

Join the Email List 

Sign up for the email list and the merchant will make sure you know when it runs Samsung sales and promos. It’ll also send you Samsung discount codes when they’re available.

Where to Find Samsung Deals and Offers

  • All Deals Page - Navigate to the All Deals page to check out all the available offers, discounts and coupon codes. You can find nice discounts on anything from large and small appliances to tech.
  • Cell Phone Offers - Head to the Cell Phone Deals page. Here, you’ll find tons of offers on smartphones, such as offers for up to $200 in rewards points when you purchase a new mobile device and sign up for the loyalty rewards program.
  • Computing Offers - Head to this part of the website to see the latest ways to save on computers. You can sometimes find Samsung coupons for free headphones when you make a qualifying computer or tablet purchase.
  • Appliance and Smart Home Offers - This is the easiest place to find the latest offers on appliances and smart tech. The merchant also puts all its reduced-priced selections at the bottom of the page.
  • TV and Home Theater Offers - Check out the TV Deals section. Here you can find the latest deals specific to Samsung televisions and home theater products.

Samsung may not offer printable coupons or printable promo codes at this time, so take advantage of the online coupons and online promo codes that are offered seasonally for most electronics and appliances. 

Trade-In Deals

  • TV Trade-In Program - Depending on the television you have and its condition, you might be able to score up to $200 in credit to apply toward lowering the cost of a new TV or home theater system
  • Mobile Trade-In Program - Send back your old smartphone, and you might qualify for up to $300 in credit you can use to lower the cost of a new phone

Samsung Financing

The company offers attractive financing deals that’ll help you save a bundle on TVs, cameras and more. You can sometimes score interest-free financing for up to 36 months depending on the product you purchase and how much you spend. You can also take part in the store’s mobile and TV upgrade programs if you shop with Samsung Financing.

Samsung Rewards 

Join the merchant’s loyalty rewards program and earn up to 2% back in rewards when you shop for electronics. Use your accumulated points to reduce the cost of your next purchase from Samsung and maximize your online deals. 

Discount Program

The store offers reduced prices to members of several organizations, military service members, students, professors, teachers and government workers. It does not offer senior citizen discounts at this time. Sign up with an email address from a qualifying institution and the store will send you more information about the savings program. It also lets you stack these coupons on top of other Samsung deals.

Rebates

Hit the Rebates page to find ways to get cash back or gift cards after you purchase a Samsung appliance.
