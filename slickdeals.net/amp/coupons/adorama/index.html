About Adorama

Adorama sells a wide variety of electronics, but they are best known for their great selection of cameras, lenses and photo accessories. You'll find whatever you need at Adorama, whether you are an audiophile, tech geek, a beginner photographer or an expert photographer. In addition to their great selection and reasonable prices, you can save even more at Adorama by using their many in-store coupons, online discounts, rebates and loyalty programs. You can expect both item and department-specific coupons, as well as sitewide discounts. Additionally, their clearance and refurbished deals often boast savings upwards of 60% off.

Adorama Discount Tips

How to Use an Adorama Coupon

 

To get the best online deals by using a coupon code, first you have to add your items to your cart and select “Checkout.” Fill out your shipping information and then scroll down to the “Payment” section. Click where it says “Do you have a gift card or promo code?” Enter your gift card and/or promo code in the appropriate box and click “Apply” on the respective boxes. Your total will then reflect any discounts that you have received thanks to your online coupon. It doesn’t look like there are any printable coupon options or in-store promo codes, as you are already getting the best in-store deals.

Shipping is not included in your subtotal, so make sure you are accounting for the shipping price. Many items qualify for free shipping, sometimes even expedited free shipping. You can learn more about eligibility by visiting Adorama's shipping policy page.

While there are plenty of coupon codes to choose from, Adorama does not support stackable coupons at this time. 

How to Save Money at Adorama

Even if there are no store-specific deals or discounts available, there are plenty of other ways to get the best bang for your buck at Adorama. Shop Adorama's huge used and refurbished collection of products, which are great in-store discounts with numerous, affordable warranty-protected options to choose from. You can also sell your old used cameras and accessories to Adorama. This is a simple way to get instant credit towards something new without the hassle of listing and selling the item independently.

Sign Up For Adorama’s Reward Program

Sign up for Adorama's VIP360 program for $49.99 a year. Once enrolled in the VIP30 loyalty program, you'll get the insider's edge to saving: you’ll get emails about new products exclusive to VIP360 members, VIP360-only discounts, special promos as well as 20GB of free cloud storage to store photos, videos, and documents. These major discounts will bee especially handy during the holidays. Plus, you’ll get access to free photography eBooks and member-only contests. Adorama does not have a dedicated email program, so this is the best way to stay on top of Adorama printable promo codes, online promo codes and other special online discounts.

Check Out Adorama’s Overstock Pages

Visit Adorama’s overstock pages for hundreds of new cameras, lenses, bags and other accessories with their prices slashed to clear warehouse space. Naturally, some of these items will be a little bit older than their higher-priced, more recently-released counterparts — but it's a haven for premium, well-received camera products being offered at a discount, savings that only grow with an Adorama coupon applied to your order.

Get Cash Back On Your Adorama Purchase

Search Adorama’s Rebate Center to net more than $300 in savings depending on what you’re buying. A quick search of the Rebate center will show you all the products they currently have with a rebate, which range from the typical check-in-the-mail deals, cash back or offers for valuable rewards like gift cards and future discounts.

Take Advantage of Adorama Rentals

You can try out the gear first, and then use the credit from your rental to purchase the item. All you have to do is purchase the item you rented within 30 days and you’ll receive 100% back of up to one week’s rental fee, which will apply to your purchase.

Sign Up For the Adorama Credit Card 

If you plan on making a large purchase (or several) at Adorama, you can get a line of credit to help finance your purchase. The Adorama credit card allows six-month financing on purchases between $500 and $999, and 12-month financing for purchases over $1,000.

Adorama Return Policy and Price Adjustments 

Thanks to their reputation as a marketplace for reselling and renting used equipment, returning your electronics to Adorama is pretty simple. As long as you return your item within 30 days of original purchase, you will be refunded (minus the cost of shipping).

Similarly, they have a 30 day price guarantee. If you find an item at a better price than what you bought it for, Adorama will give you a price adjustment. 

You Can Get Special Discounts Based On Your Status 

Even if there are no deals or discounts running at Adorama, some groups can save automatically. They offer a student discount and educator discount that provides discounts on thousands of products, as well as expedited shipping. Students and educators must sign up for the program. 

It does not look like Adorama offers a senior citizen discount or a military discount at this time. 

Contact Adorama Customer Service 

If you have any questions about your order, coupons or anything else, it is easy to get in touch with the Adorama customer service team. You can contact their customer service phone number by calling 1 (800) 815-0702. To access live chat and the Adorama Help Center, you can visit their contact us page.
