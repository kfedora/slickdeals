Home Depot Discount Tips

Home Depot often offers discounts throughout their different departments, so it's always worth checking for promo codes and price reductions on power tools, home decor, flooring, appliances and more.

Ways To Save at Home Depot

They have Special Buy of the Day deals, where you can get certain items at deep discounts, no coupon code needed. You can access these deals online (with free shipping!) or in-store. These deals are usually limited time only, so make sure to pounce while your desired items are in stock.

Signing up for emails or text messages will earn you $5 off your next purchase of $50 or more, plus exclusive Home Depot coupon codes, promotions, and offers.

Military members and their families can get 10 percent off any day by showing a military ID. 

Be sure to keep this page bookmarked to find all the current Home Depot coupons, promo codes and specials.

Overstock Inventory

In addition to their regular specials, you can save big by purchasing from Home Depot’s overstock inventory, which frequently boasts discounts of up to 50 percent off normal retail price. The Home Depot Rebate Center is also great for finding hundreds of dollars in cash back on select tools and appliances. If you're torn between the Ryobi versus the Dewalt drill and neither have a coupon code, check and see who has a rebate.

Does Home Depot Have Free Shipping and In Store Pickup?

Looking for free shipping? Just spend more than $45 or ship items to your local store for free pick up. You can also opt to have items sent directly to your home, which may become free with if you spend above the minimum and/or use Home Depot promo codes. Items like appliances and select furniture usually qualify for free delivery.

If you don't want to wait for two-day shipping or don't qualify for free delivery, you can instead buy online and pick up in store. Once you checkout online, your order will be ready at your local Home Depot in about two hours. 

Home Depot Coupon Policy

Home Depot’s competitor coupon policy no longer honors competitor coupon codes, but price matching is still available. If you find a better deal on an identical product at another local competitor, Home Depot’s low price guarantee will match the price and then give you another 10% off that price. All you need is the proof of price, no code needed. Unfortunately, sale items are excluded from price matching.

You can not stack Home Depot coupons. Generally speaking, Home Depot coupons are only valid for a single receipt in-store purchase, and usually only up to $200 off unless otherwise noted. Even so, a combination of price-matched select items, rebates, sale items, Special Buys, coupons and Deals of the Day will shave a substantial amount off of your Home Depot purchase. 

Home Depot Credit Card

If you have a big project coming up, a Home Depot consumer credit card might be a good idea as special promotions and offers are sent to card members throughout the year. Special financing is also available for those who qualify.

More About Home Depot Coupons

Does Home Depot offer military or senior discounts?

Home Depot does not offer any senior or military discounts on-line. However, active duty military members can show their ID in-store and get a 10% discount on their purchase.

What is Home Depots price matching & return policy?

They offer a 'Low Price Guarantee' for both in-store and online purchases. For online purchases the person purchasing must submit the lower price found to a representative. Customers can bring a print out or cut out of the ad promoting a lower price of an identical item in-store before purchasing.

Does Home Depot have a rewards program or credit card offer?

There are several credit card offers that customers can apply for, each giving a different financing promotion to accommodate different projects: their standard Consumer Credit Card, Project Loan card, Commercial Revolving Charge Card and Commercial Charge card.

What is Home depot's free shipping policy?

Customers can get free standard shipping from Home Depot for most orders above $45 as well as free shipping on appliances above $396.
