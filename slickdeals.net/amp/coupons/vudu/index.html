About VUDU

Vudu is a subscription-free, contract-free streaming service. It has over 100,000 movies and TV shows to rent or buy, and many are available in 4K UHD. The company also boasts having popular and sought after content before it becomes available on competing streaming services. Vudu works across many types of devices, such as smart TVs, game consoles, streaming services like Roku and most mobile devices, so you can watch your favorite TV shows and movies from anywhere. With a reasonably priced selection and an impressive library of free content, it seems improbable that Vudu could somehow be more cost effective. However, you can knock a few dollars (or your entire total) off of premium Vudu content by using the streaming service’s online promo codes and coupon codes. When you know how to make the most out of Vudu’s deals and promos, you can cut out the pesky monthly subscription fees and enjoy all of your favorite titles on demand—and on a budget that you can afford.

VUDU Discount Tips

Vudu Discount Tips 

Pay less for your favorite movies, TV series and TV shows with all of the online deals and discounts that Vudu has to offer. You're already saving by watching movies and shows from home, and you can save even more with these money-saving tips on your next digital movie download when you come prepared with the latest online coupons and discount codes. Even when there aren’t any Vudu codes available, you can still save money on movie rentals by opting for a Bundle and Save deal or browsing Vudu’s selection of Free content.

Vudu does not offer any status-based discounts like a student discount, military discount or senior citizen discount. This streaming service does offer a sale on battle and military themed movies and TV shows on Veteran’s Day, but there are no online discounts dedicated to any particular group. 

Vudu Coupon Policy 

If you find yourself in possession of a Vudu promo code or voucher code, you can redeem it at the Vudu Code Redemption Center. Simply click on the Redeem Vudu Code link at the bottom of the Vudu homepage or click the Redeem link at the top of any Movie or TV page to use your Vudu coupon code.

Vudu does not allow the use of stackable coupons. Make sure you’ve chosen the right promo code for your purchase before you complete your order. 

Shop Vudu Deals and Sales

Browse on weekends as well as around holidays and between seasons to get great Vudu deals. Offers have included a summer sale on new releases, a summer gaming sale on video game-inspired titles and even a WWII-themed sale. You can easily find free or discounted TV episodes, discounted rentals and monthly deals for under $20. You can also save by renting for a shorter time—for example, you can often purchase a two-day rental for $2.

Bundle and Save

Check for the Bundle & Save label as you browse Vudu. Often you can get multiple movies or an entire TV show season for less than buying them separately. Offers have included a Black Panther plus Captain America Civil War bundle, a Blade Runner three-film collection and 24: The Complete Series.

Subscribe to the Vudu Email Program

Click on the Sign In link on Vudu's homepage and select Create an Account. With just your name and email address, or when you sign in with your Walmart account, you can start watching Vudu's free content. Once you’ve created an account, you should enable email updates to ensure that you don’t miss out on any new releases, special promotions and other exclusive subscriber-only content. As a welcome gift for joining, you'll get your first rental for just $0.99. Once you create an account, you'll gain access to the Movie On Us section, which includes movies, TV series and TV shows in categories such as Family and Kids, Action, Comedy and much more.

Vudu does not have a rewards program or loyalty program. By creating an account on the site and enabling email updates, you can rest assured knowing that you’re always getting the best possible deal on your rentals. 

Vudu Return Policy and Price Adjustment Policy 

Vudu has a unique return policy, as this streaming platform allows its users to request a "Rental Redo" if you decide that the movie you selected isn't what you wanted. You have to decide within the first 30 minutes to qualify, and you can only use the Rental Redo feature four times a month.

Vudu does not offer price adjustments on its own titles, but it will price match Amazon Video, iTunes and other similar streaming services. 

Get the Best Shipping Price

Since all of Vudu’s services are rendered entirely through the internet, you will never have to worry about scraping the internet for a free shipping promo code. Money that could be spent on physical copies and related shipping costs can be spent on your favorite movies and TV shows instead! 

Contact Vudu Customer Service

Have questions about your Vudu rental, the Rental Redo policy or any other related inquiries? There are a couple of different ways to get in touch with the company. The Vudu customer service phone number is 1 (888) 554-VUDU. If you would prefer to send an email, you can select the applicable support email address from Vudu's Contact Us page.
