20 Stores With Prime Day Sales That Feel Like Early Black Friday Deals

1 Day Ago by Isis Briones Contributor
At Slickdeals, we work hard to help you find the best deals. To do this, some of the products featured here are from our partners, who may provide us with compensation. However, this doesn’t influence our opinions. Our views are our own.

Even though there's no denying that it's stacked with savings, Amazon Prime Day isn't the only place you can find solid deals online. While you can get virtually any brand delivered to your door via Amazon, these same companies want to make sure you're going to them first for exclusive sale prices on their items.

Slick Tip: If there's a specific store you want to shop, just click over to their site before automatically going to Amazon. Depending on the brand, you have a strong chance of landing a better deal when purchasing directly from the company.

From Ulta to Reebok, adidas and more, you'll be surprised at all the sales you won't find on Amazon Prime Day. On top of that, other major e-commerce competitors including Target and Walmart, among many others, are basically throwing early Black Friday deals at customers to get them to shop outside of Amazon. Scroll through below to see exactly what you're missing out on by just focusing on Amazon.

Shop These Non-Amazon Stores Offering Great Early Black Friday Deals

1. adidas

Whether you're looking to up your sneaker game with a fresh pair of Yeezys or are simply in need of cold-weather workout gear, adidas is coming through just in time for adidas' Creators Club members-only sale.

Don't worry, you can instantly sign up for a free membership. Once you're all set, you'll be able to add the exclusive promo code CREATE to receive an additional 33% off full-price and sale items.

Slick Tip: When you subscribe to the Creators Club, know that you'll also be sent a 15% off coupon to use for future purchases.

2. Allswell

The popular mattress company is providing customers with discounts on the brand's fall-favorites collection. You'll be able to get 30% off select styles but only 15% off the luxe and supreme mattresses. All you have to do is add the coupon code FALLFAVES at checkout.

3. Anthropologie

Anthropologie is known for adding sales on top of items that are already on clearance, and things are no different on Amazon Prime Day. For a limited time, bargain hunters can get an additional 25% off all sale clothing on the site.

4. Bed Bath & Beyond

Get excited because Bed Bath & Beyond is having a surprise two-day savings event with discounts of up to 30% off. Additionally, if you spend at least $200, you'll get $50 back in rewards.

Slick Tip: Don't forget, if you sign up for Bed Bath & Beyond's e-mail newsletter, you'll get a 20% off coupon that can be used towards any single item along with free shipping on all orders over $39.

5. Best Buy

It looks like Best Buy fast-forwarded to Thanksgiving this week as the brand officially announced an Early Black Friday Sale. You better act fast because these prices will only run from today to tomorrow (October 13-14).

6. Boll & Branch

Considering all the extra time you're spending at home nowadays, you could probably use a luxury sheets upgrade. Well, you're in luck as you can get an additional 15% off an order of $150 or more for a limited time at Boll & Branch.

Slick Tip: Do you not currently have the budget for it? Don't stress. When you're ready, just add your e-mail to the brand's newsletter, and you'll instantly be sent a 10% off coupon — which can be used on a purchase of $200 or more.

7. Brooklinen

Interested in another sheets option? Turn to Brooklinen; the site is having a Surprise Savings Event that gives customers 15% off all their best-selling products sitewide. To make things extra easy, there's no code required for the discount.

8.Crate & Barrel

To make room for next year's new releases, Crate & Barrel is completely slashing prices on last season's clearance items. All items are up to 70% off, so take advantage of them all while supplies last.

9. Dyson

Nothing beats a squeaky clean home, and Dyson's high-quality products can get you there. On top of getting $150 off the popular Dyson Cyclone V10 Absolute, you can also save $70 on an array of air purifiers.

10. Lucky Brand

Enjoy an extra 40% off during Lucky brand's sitewide sale in addition to free shipping. Deals include women's tees for just $6 and a men's Lucky brand polo for just $9. Like with most online stores and brands, you can also trade your email for an extra 15% off (qualifying orders).

11. Nespresso

Elevate your mornings with a coveted Nespresso machine, and for now, owning one won't break the bank. Coffee lovers can save 25% off the brand's Vertuo machines.

Slick Tip: Shipping is free beginning at $35. And they'll send you free coffee samples!

12. Outdoor Voices

The Outdoor Voices' OV Extra Sale is back, which means you can get an extra 50% off select styles. There's nothing better than getting half off, so get to these products before your size runs out.

Slick Tip: Get $10 off your first order by providing your email.

13. Puma

Want to follow in Selena Gomez's footsteps? You can shop the star's entire collection and more at 25% off with the promo code FLASH25.

Slick Tip: To make sure you get an extra discount on future orders, join the Puma fam's e-mail newsletter to get a 10% off coupon.

14. Reebok

From now until October 18, Reebok isn't messing around. The Cardi B-approved footwear and clothing brand is doing a 50% off sitewide promotion with code HELLYEAH50. Go ahead and grab those sneaks you've been wanting all year long.

15. Samsung

Similar to Best Buy, Samsung is here with early Black Friday prices. Customers can get up to $1,300 off a new entertainment system like the 75-inch Terrace Smart TV that's been marked down from $6,499.99 to $5,199.99.

Slick Tip: You don't have to pay for your purchase all at once; Samsung has payment options. For instance, you can pay $108.33 a month for 48 months for the Terrace Smart TV mentioned above.

16. Shopbop

Shopbop technically is owned by Amazon, but the brand can get buried under all the Prime Day buzz. The stand-alone trendy style site is having its own fall sales event where the more you shop, the more you'll save. You can save 15% on orders over $200, 20% on orders over $500 and 25% off on orders over $800 with code FALL20. If you've been eyeing that designer item, this might be the time to go for it.

Slick Tip: Plus, save 15% on your first purchase by signing up with email.

17. Tatcha

Tatcha (the cult Japanese skincare line beauty influencers wear by) is promoting a Friends and Family sale. For a limited time, you and your loved ones can get up to 20% off sitewide with code FF2020.

Slick Tip: While not as good as some other newsletter perks, you'll receive a free mini Dewy Skin Cream with your first order when you provide your email.

18. Target

If you head over to Target, you'll find some standout deals including $150 off Beats Studio3 headphones, up to 40% off furniture and so much more.

19. Walmart

In an effort to pull customers away from Amazon Prime Day, Walmart is offering a similar free-two day shipping perk on all orders over $35. Do some price comparisons because once you qualify for free shipping, it's possible you could get a better price at Walmart.

20. Wayfair

Wayfair is always having a sale, so it's no surprise that the site is having a 48-Hour Clearance event. Get household essentials at up to 70% off, and give your space the ultimate refresh for less.
