When Was the Walmart Black Friday Ad Scan Released?

  • November 13th, 2020

As we predicted, the 2020 Walmart ad scan was released late in the evening on Wednesday, November 13th. The store has a history of leaking its ad on Wednesday nights. (It did so on November 7th last year and November 8th in 2017.)

What Time Does Walmart Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving?

  • Walmart will open its doors at 6 p.m. Thursday, November 28th
  • Prices are valid all day Friday, as well, with store hours varying by location

Once again, Walmart is opening its doors at 6 p.m on Thanksgiving Day. Friday's hours will vary, so be sure to check with your local Walmart before heading out. Many Walmart locations have simply remained open through Friday in years past.

Key departments, such as toys, electronics, home and apparel, will be color-coded throughout the store with balloons and signs to help you navigate the sale. Plus, customers can download the Walmart mobile app for an interactive guide to all the deals.

When Does the Walmart Black Friday Sale Begin Online?

  • Walmart's online sale will kick off at 10 p.m. ET on Wednesday, November 27th

Walmart's online sale officially starts on Wednesday night, with deals going live at 10 p.m. ET. For those of you who love Black Friday but hate the crowds, some late-night online shopping may be the perfect solution.

With that said, the store is already offering several early Black Friday deals online — including some pretty big discounts on 4K TVs from leading brands like Samsung, LG and TCL. If you're shopping for a new TV this holiday season, we suggest taking a look.

Walmart's free two-day shipping includes millions of items, but you'll still need to make a $35 minimum purchase for the free shipping to apply (or just ship your order to your local store to avoid the shipping charges altogether).

When Is the Best Time to Shop?

  • November 27th starting at 10 p.m. ET online (or 6 p.m. Thanksgiving Day for in-store-only deals)

With deals kicking off early this year, shopping from home is probably your best strategy. That being said, some items may sell out online relatively fast — and some items are in-store only. If a trip to the store is necessary to get your holiday must-haves, then plan on arriving before doors open at 6 p.m. on Thanksgiving.

More Ways to Save at Walmart on Black Friday

Relying on store-offered discounts isn't the only way to save. Here are a few other money-saving hacks to help you get the best possible deals during the Walmart Black Friday sale.

  • Take advantage of gift card offers. This year, the store is giving customers Walmart gift cards worth up to $700 when they purchase select smartphones via payment plans, which is $300 more than the store offered last year. This deal is definitely worth a look if you're due for an upgrade.
  • Score a pickup discount. Ordering your Black Friday items online and having them shipped to your nearest store could keep some extra cash in your pocket. Just look for the "Pickup Discount" savings under the digital price tag at checkout.
  • Download the mobile app. Venturing out to the store? Be sure to download the Walmart mobile app to access a Black Friday map that makes finding the best deals that much easier. Just browse the Black Friday circular on the Walmart app, select an item and its location will appear on the Black Friday Store Map during the sale.
  • Consider a Walmart credit card. New applicants can currently earn 5% back on Walmart purchases for the first 12 months when they use their Capital One Walmart Rewards Card. As with all credit cards, just make sure you pay off your full balance every month to avoid interest charges.
