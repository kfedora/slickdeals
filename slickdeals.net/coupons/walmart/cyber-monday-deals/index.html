When Was the Walmart Cyber Monday Ad Released?

  • November 13th, 2020

As we predicted, the 2020 Walmart ad scan was released late in the evening on Wednesday, November 13th. The store has a history of leaking its ad on Wednesday nights. (It did so on November 7th last year and November 8th in 2017.)

When Does the Walmart Cyber Monday Sale Begin Online?

  • Walmart's online sale will kick off at 10 p.m. ET on Wednesday, November 27th

Walmart's online sale officially starts on Wednesday night, with deals going live at 10 p.m. ET. For those of you who love Black Friday but hate the crowds, some late-night online shopping may be the perfect solution.

With that said, the store already offered several early Cyber Monday online deals — including some pretty big discounts on 4K TVs from leading brands like Samsung, LG and TCL. If you're shopping for a new TV this holiday season, we suggest taking a look.

When Is the Best Time to Shop?

  • November 27th starting at 10 p.m. ET online (or 6 p.m. Thanksgiving Day for in-store-only deals)

With deals kicking off early this year, shopping from home is probably your best strategy. That being said, some items may sell out online relatively fast — and some items are in-store only. If a trip to the store is necessary to get your holiday must-haves, then plan on arriving before doors open at 6 p.m. on Thanksgiving.

More Ways to Save at Walmart on Cyber Monday

Relying on store-offered discounts isn't the only way to save. Here are a few other money-saving hacks to help you get the best possible deals during the Walmart Cyber Monday sale.

  • Take advantage of gift card offers. This year, the store is giving customers Walmart gift cards worth up to $700 when they purchase select smartphones via payment plans, which is $300 more than the store offered last year. This deal is definitely worth a look if you're due for an upgrade.
  • Score a pickup discount. Ordering your Cyber Monday items online and having them shipped to your nearest store could keep some extra cash in your pocket. Just look for the "Pickup Discount" savings under the digital price tag at checkout.
  • Download the mobile app. Venturing out to the store? Be sure to download the Walmart mobile app to access a Cyber Monday map that makes finding the best deals that much easier. Just browse the Cyber Monday circular on the Walmart app, select an item and its location will appear on the Cyber Monday Store Map during the sale.
  • Consider a Walmart credit card. New applicants can currently earn 5% back on Walmart purchases for the first 12 months when they use their Capital One Walmart Rewards Card. As with all credit cards, just make sure you pay off your full balance every month to avoid interest charges.

2020 Walmart Cyber Monday Sale Tips

Don't miss knockout bargains in every department during the 2020 Walmart Cyber Monday sale. Use the tips below to save your hard-earned bucks.

Save More on Select Products

Each year Walmart chooses a few select items to feature on Cyber Monday. These items vary from year to year but are usually highly anticipated by shoppers. For example, the 2018 sale included savings on Nintendo 2DS XL, 4K TVs, gaming laptops and treadmills. Typically they discount big-ticket items, though many cheaper products are discounted storewide as well. In 2018 shoppers found a Dyson V-6 Handheld Vacuum for half price and a Samsung 56-Inch 4K TV at a huge discount.

Sign Up for the Newsletter

Sign up for the Walmart newsletter if you want to get updates as soon as this year's deals are available. The publication includes information about new arrivals, exclusive deals and upcoming sales like Cyber Monday. Provide your email address to opt-in. It's completely free and you don't need to make a purchase to qualify for sale alerts.

Follow Walmart on Social Media

Walmart is on several social media platforms. You can follow their profiles on Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest, YouTube and Instagram. They often post updates on their upcoming sales events and new products. We expect that they'll post updates on the upcoming Walmart Cyber Monday sales 2020 event as well, so follow them for all the latest news and be ready to save big and often.
