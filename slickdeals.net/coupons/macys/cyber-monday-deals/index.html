  • Enroll in Star Rewards ASAP. Macy's loyalty program lets you earn discounts toward future purchases based on how much you spend. The store's cardholders are automatically enrolled in Star Rewards as Silver-tier members, but you don't need a Macy's credit card to join. Anyone can enroll online, in-store or over the phone and become a Bronze member of Star Rewards for free. Members who also use the Macy’s credit card get free shipping with no spending minimum.
  • Buy discounted gift cards in advance. Now is the time to purchase discounted Macy’s gift cards and merchandise return credit through certified websites and eBay accounts. You can save 5-10% off your total order by buying a discounted card.
  • Pick up your items in-store. Spending less than $49? Skip the shipping charge by selecting free in-store pickup at checkout.
  • Buy a beauty item to get free shipping. Another way to get your order shipped free is to add a beauty item to your cart. All beauty orders ship free of charge.

Macy's Cyber Monday FAQ

Is Macy's having a Cyber Monday sale?

Yes. Last year, Macy's held a Cyber Week sale. In 2020, it will at least be hosted December 1–December 2.

What time does Cyber Monday start for Macy's?

In prior years, the sale started online at 12:00 AM local time on Monday.

Does Macy's price match on Cyber Monday?

Yes. Macy's price matches a few stores. Check with the site for items that are eligible for price matching.
