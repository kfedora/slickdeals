When Was the 2020 Costco Black Friday Ad Released?

  • October 30th, 2020

Costco's Black Friday ad leaked this year on October 30th, which is a few days later than its 2018 reveal. Still, it's the first official Black Friday ad scan of 2020. The 32-page circular is far bigger than last year's 8-page scan and showcases a wide range of in-store and online deals to expect now through December 2nd.

What Time Does Costco Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving 2020?

  • Closed on Thanksgiving Day; stores open at 9 a.m. on Black Friday

Costco's brick-and-mortar locations are once again closed on Thanksgiving Day, but they re-open on Black Friday at 9 a.m. Closing times will stay the same (8:30 p.m. on weekdays and 6 p.m. on weekends). The deals will continue while supplies last through Monday, December 2nd.

When Does the Sale Begin Online at Costco.com?

  • Thanksgiving Day starting at midnight (although select deals are available online as early as October 30th)

Costco may be closed on Thanksgiving, but the store's online sale officially kicks off on Thanksgiving Day. Some deals (like the Xbox bundle) are even available as early as November 24th. Keep in mind that members-only items aren't available to non-members online, but everything else is shoppable for members and non-members alike.

Which Deals Should You Focus on at Costco This Black Friday?

Home goods and electronics are typically the stand-outs from Costco on Black Friday, and this year is no exception. We're also spotting some slick price breaks on 4K TVs, although Sam's Club has comparable if not better prices on certain models included in its upcoming One-Day Sale on November 9th. Walmart's Black Friday ad leak is right around the corner, so here's hoping we'll see more and more price drops on these big-ticket items.

Before you jump at any early-start holiday deals, it's smart to hold off and see what's included in competitors' ads. Stores like Walmart, Target, Macy's and Kohl's should have lots of discounts on kitchen appliances, vacuums and gaming consoles. Similarly, Best Buy will likely have excellent deals on smart home devices, while Lowe's will offer low prices on home appliances.

Slickdealer ahzroe thinks that "either of the Sonos soundbars looks promising," and many of our forum users raved that this brand is worth the investment.

User sdtacos250 adds that the "Verizon Note 10+ for $630 looks nice" — and we agree big time. You can purchase the Verizon Samsung Galaxy S10, S10+ or Note10+ for $429.99 – $629.99, plus you'll get a $420 Costco Shop Card with your activation.

That being said, not everyone was impressed. looser24 felt like "it's almost as if they raised prices for Black Friday."

When is The Best Time to Shop?

  • Online: midnight on Thanksgiving Day
  • Brick-and-mortar: as early as possible on Black Friday

The best time to shop the Costco Black Friday sale largely depends on what you're buying, but shopping online is generally your best bet. Items are available in limited quantities, and most offers are available online starting on Thanksgiving Day, so it makes sense to stay home and shop Costco.com while enjoying your pumpkin pie.

If you'd rather brave the rush and visit the store in-person, get there as soon as doors open on Black Friday itself. Deals on TVs and laptops are usually the most popular options, and it's likely the electronics with the biggest discounts will go first.

More Ways to Save at Costco on Black Friday

Shopping at Costco—at least in person—requires a membership. If you splurge for either the Business Executive ($120) or the Gold Star Executive ($120) memberships, you get extra benefits on select Costco services.

Both the Business Executive and the Gold Star Executive memberships provide an annual 2% reward on eligible Costco and Costco travel purchases. College students can join as regular members to receive a $10 Costco cash card or as executive members to receive a $20 Costco cash card. Additionally, members of the military will receive special offers.

That being said, there is a way around the membership fee altogether: Costco Cash Cards. A Costco member will have to purchase one for you or re-load an existing gift card, but using one lets you score members-only prices both in-store and online! One drawback? The costs are 5% higher online when you don't have an actual membership.
