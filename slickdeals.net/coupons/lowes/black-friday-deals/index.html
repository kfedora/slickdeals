When Was the Lowe's Black Friday Ad Released?

  • November 14th, 2020

Lowe's released its Black Friday ad scan on Thursday, November 14th, just a few short weeks away from Black Friday itself. Doorbusters dominate the first page, and some of the less-exciting deals and price decreases are already live.

What Time Does Lowe's Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving 2020?

  • Closed on Thanksgiving, open on Black Friday starting at 6 a.m.

Enjoy the whole of Thanksgiving with your family, but be prepared to rise and shine early if you want to get in on Lowe's doorbusters. Lowe's opens at 6 a.m. on Black Friday, and starts strong with a deal that offers the first 300 in-store shoppers a chance to win a $500 Lowe's gift card.

The same shoppers will also get a $10 off $50+ coupon for Black Friday shopping.

When Does the Sale Begin Online?

  • Some deals are already available — others will go live on Black Friday starting at 6 a.m.

Certain Lowe's offerings for Black Friday are already available online. However, the official online sale should start at the same time as the Black Friday sale itself — 6 a.m. on November 29th.

When is the Best Time to Shop?

  • Several hours after opening, or online Black Friday morning, as early as 6 a.m.

While everyone else is fighting the early morning Black Friday crowds over at the electronics stores, your best bet is shopping at home in your PJs on Friday the 29th. Alternatively, head to Lowe's within a couple hours of opening. Most bargains will still be available well after the sun is up, and you can spend more time crossing items off your shopping list (and not fighting for a parking spot).

More Ways to Save at Lowe's on Black Friday

  • The Lowe's Advantage credit card offers 5% off your purchases, but make sure to read all the terms and conditions before you apply to see if it's right for you.
  • Remember to compare competitors' prices. Lowe's Price Match Guarantee is available in-store on Black Friday, which means you could score an extra discount if you find a lower price elsewhere.
  • Lowe's offers a 10% discount for all active military personnel and veterans. Once you register, you can use the discount online or in-store. Just be sure to check with your local store to see if the discount applies on Black Friday.
  • Check Lowe's rebate center to find or track rebates for the items you've purchased.
  • Get free shipping when you spend at least $45 or opt for free store pick up.
