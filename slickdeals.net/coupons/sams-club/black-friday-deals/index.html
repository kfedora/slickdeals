What Time Does Sam's Club Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving?

  • Stores will be closed on Thanksgiving and open at 7 a.m. on Black Friday, November 29th

Brick-and-mortar Sam's Club locations are closed once again this Thanksgiving, but stores will open bright and early at 7 a.m. on Black Friday.

The sale runs through the entire weekend with prices valid until December 1st or while supplies last. Many of the sales will also be available online at SamsClub.com with free shipping options during the sale.

When Does the Sale Begin Online?

  • 12:01 a.m. EST on Thursday, November 28th

Just like last year, Sam's Club Black Friday sale will kick off early online at 12:01 a.m. EST on Thanksgiving Day. All of the top doorbusters are available online (and they all ship for free), so you can avoid the crowds by shopping at SamsClub.com/BlackFriday instead.

When is The Best Time to Shop?

  • Online, as early as possible

Sam's Club's doorbuster deals will all be available online, so shopping from home on Thanksgiving Day is likely your best bet. Still, there is a good chance that some of the doorbusters will sell out online, which might mean a trip to the store on Friday is in order.

With that in mind, if you're a brick-and-mortar-type shopper, make sure you're at Sam's Club well ahead of the crowd. If you prefer to sit at home and shop in leisure, you'll want to be on top of that online sale as early as possible.

More Ways to Save at Sam's Club on Black Friday

While you do need a Sam’s Club membership to shop in-store, you don’t need a membership to shop online. The only downside to this is that non-members are charged a 10% service fee.

If you are interested in a membership, prices start at $45. Many times, Sam’s Club has membership bundles on sale. These bundles usually let new members purchase a membership for $45 and receive a bonus gift card and free food items.

If you are planning on getting a big item, such as a TV or laptop, it might be worthwhile to invest in the Plus membership. Upgrade your membership on top of a bundled deal to get the maximum benefit. Plus members earn cashback on their purchases, and select TVs and laptops earn 3X cashback during the Black Friday sale.
