How Long Does the Sam's Club Cyber Week Sale Last?

  • December 2nd through December 8th

Just like last year, Sam's Club's Cyber Week sale will run from Cyber Monday through the following Sunday (December 8th). All of the top doorbusters are available online (and most ship for free).

Keep in mind, however, that some of the most exciting deals are limited to Cyber Monday. Other deals might only run a few days and end on December 4th or 6th. Make sure to check the expiration dates on discounts you're excited about.

When is The Best Time to Shop?

  • Online, as early as possible

Sam's Club's doorbuster deals will all be available online, but popular items can sell out fast. If you have your eye on something, don't assume it'll still be there a day later.

More Ways to Save at Sam's Club During Cyber Week

While you do need a Sam’s Club membership to shop in-store, you don’t need a membership to shop online. The only downside to this is that non-members are charged a 10% service fee.

If you are interested in a membership, prices start at $45. Many times, Sam’s Club has membership bundles on sale. These bundles usually let new members purchase a membership for $45 and receive a bonus gift card and free food items.

If you are planning on getting a big item, such as a TV or laptop, it might be worthwhile to invest in the Plus membership. Upgrade your membership on top of a bundled deal to get the maximum benefit.
