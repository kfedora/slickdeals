When Will the Amazon Black Friday Sale Begin?

  • Last year, Amazon's Black Friday sale began on Friday, November 16th and continued through Black Friday, November 23rd.
  • Expect "Early Black Friday Deals" to become available online roughly a week before November 29th.
  • Prime members may be granted even earlier access to select deals.

Amazon switched things up a bit in 2018 by introducing "Amazon’s Seven Days of Black Friday Deals." If Amazon decides to make the event a tradition this year, you'll be able to browse thousands of Black Friday deals before most retailers open their doors.

For 2018, Amazon also offered 50+ "Deals of the Day" in the week leading up to Black Friday, so keep checking back to see what new offers are available.

When Is The Best Time to Shop?

  • Black Friday and Cyber Monday will likely feature some of the best deals, but be on the lookout for solid discounts earlier in the week, too.

Last year, Amazon broke down the Black Friday season into three distinct events: pre-Black Friday sales, Black Friday itself and Cyber Monday. Previous years suggest the Black Friday and Cyber Monday deals will be pretty similar, especially when it comes to sales on Amazon devices.

The pre-Black Friday deals are a bit more hit or miss, as you never really know what items are going to be featured. While you should definitely pop in early to see what offers are available, you might want to curb your enthusiasm a bit — the best values rarely present themselves in the event's first hours.

Since the pre-Black Friday deals will probably change every day, we recommend filtering deals on our Amazon store page or setting up Deal Alerts for the products you're most interested in to get notified about flash sales.

More Ways to Save at Amazon During Black Friday

These tips and hacks wll help you save even more during Amazon's Black Friday 2020 event:

  • When shopping for toys and apparel, be patient. Amazon has historically offered better deals on these categories during Cyber Monday than on Black Friday.
  • Join Amazon Prime. If not forever, just for Black Friday. Most new members are eligible for a 30-day free trial subscription, which means you can get all the benefits of a Prime Member on Black Friday without spending a cent. These perks include access to Prime-exclusive deals, free two-day shipping and more. Just be sure to cancel your subscription before your trial is up to avoid getting auto-charged.
  • Utilize Amazon's "Watch This Deal" feature. The "Watch this Deal" button is great for receiving notifications on new deals. Just click the button, and you'll be notified when a deal pops up.
  • Keep an eye out for unique offers. In the lead up to Black Friday, Amazon will likely announce several exclusive deals. Check back with us regularly to see if Amazon has announced any unique deals for Black Friday 2020.
