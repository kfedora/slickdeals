What Time Does Best Buy Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving?

  • 5 p.m. on Thanksgiving Day, with closing around 1 a.m. (depending on your state)
  • Best Buy will likely re-open at 8 a.m. on Black Friday

While you should always contact your local Best Buy to get precise info on its Black Friday schedule, most Best Buy locations will open their doors from 5 p.m. to 1 a.m. on Thanksgiving Day.

Stores in Maine, Massachusetts and Rhode Island are the exceptions to this rule, as they are normally closed on Thanksgiving Day. They will open for business on Black Friday at 12:01 a.m. (ME) and 1 a.m. (MA and RI). For all other Best Buy locations, expect them to re-open at 8 a.m. on Black Friday.

The vast majority of online deals will go live on Thanksgiving day, though the store has not yet announced a specific time for those online sales to start.

When is the Best Time to Shop?

  • Online, as early as midnight on Thanksgiving Day
  • Around lunchtime on Black Friday at physical locations

If you're an early bird, you're going to be rewarded with a whole mess of electronic goodies come Black Friday. Even still, you'll want to be online during the early hours of Thanksgiving to make sure you don't miss this year's doorbuster deals.

If you're brave enough to do your Black Friday shopping in person and don't mind missing out on the big ticket doorbusters, you might want to wait for the crowds to disperse a bit. The least hectic time will be in the afternoon, especially around lunchtime. The mobs tend to retreat for a midday snack, giving you access to some of the less popular deals on things like Blu-rays, memory cards and other electronics that the average shopper might miss.
