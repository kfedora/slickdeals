Lids Black Friday FAQ

Can I buy Lids Black Friday deals online?

You can buy Lids Black Friday deals online through their website, or offline through a Lids retail outlet. Lids likes to run separate deals for their online shop and their physical stores so check out both to get the best deals. Get Lids Black Friday deals.

Will Lids price match Black Friday deals?

Lids items are reasonably-priced. The company doesn't usually offer price-matching deals. Get Lids Black Friday deals.

What time does Lids open on Black Friday?

The Lids online shop is open 24/7, 365 days a year but their outlet shops are usually open by 9:00 AM or 10:00 AM local time. For the official Black Friday operating hours, keep an eye out for announcements from Lids. Get Lids Black Friday deals.

What time does Lids close on Black Friday?

The Lids online shop is always open and ready to serve you. The brick-and-mortar shops have varying operating hours. Some close as early as 7:00 PM, while others as late as 12:00 AM. Find the nearest Lids store by using the Store Locator tool available at their website. Get Lids Black Friday deals.

What time do Lids Black Friday deals start?

The online Lids Black Friday deals will likely start earlier than the in-store ones. Expect online deals to go live as early as November 27, 2020, 12:01 AM PST. The offline deals will likely start when the stores open on November 29. Get Lids Black Friday deals.

What time do Black Friday Lids deals end?

Online Black Friday deals may run until November 30, 11:59 PM PST. In-store deals will likely end when the stores close on November 29, 2020. Get Lids Black Friday deals.
