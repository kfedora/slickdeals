What Time Does Kohl's Open on Black Friday and Thanksgiving?

  • Black Friday deals will be available online starting at 12:01 a.m. CT on Monday, November 25th
  • Black Friday deals will be available in stores on Thursday, November 28th starting at 5 p.m.
  • Black Friday doorbusters will be available in stores starting Thursday, November 28th at 5 p.m. and online from 12:01 a.m. CT on Thursday, November 28th until 1 p.m. on Black Friday, November 29th (or while supplies last)

For the last few years, Kohl's has offered three main opportunities for its Black Friday sale: online deals that run all week long, in-store deals that kick off on Thanksgiving Day, and highly anticipated doorbuster deals that are only available on Thursday and Friday (both in-store and online).

Kohl's will open most of its doors nationwide on Thursday, November 28th at 5 p.m. — the same schedule the retailer followed in 2018 and 2019.

Keep in mind that Kohl's is known for offering its customers extended store hours in the weeks leading up to Christmas Eve, so expect more of the same in 2020.

When Does the Kohl's Black Friday Sale Begin Online?

  • Black Friday deals will be available online starting at 12:01 a.m. CT on Monday, November 25th

The store is once again kicking off its online sale the Monday before Thanksgiving, giving shoppers a full week to enjoy Black Friday deals from the comfort of their homes.

These offers won't be available in stores until doors open on Thanksgiving at 5 p.m. Plus, this online advantage also coincides with bonus Kohl's Cash, which means customers will score $15 for every $48 they spend (instead of the typical $10).

Kohl's Black Friday doorbusters, however, aren't available in-store or online until Thanksgiving Day.

When is the Best Time to Shop?

  • Online starting Monday, November 25th if you're just looking for regular Black Friday deals
  • As early as possible on Thanksgiving Day for doorbusters, starting at 12:01 a.m. CT online or 5 p.m. in stores 

The best time to shop Kohl's Black Friday sale really depends on what you're buying. Many of the store's deals will be available online starting Monday, but if you have your eyes on specific doorbusters, you'll want to shop them as early as possible on Thanksgiving. That's when these limited-time offers go live, and some are likely to sell out fast.

You may want to consider splitting up your purchases if you plan on buying more than one thing, as well. If you shop anytime from Monday through Friday, you'll earn Kohl's Cash that you can then redeem starting Saturday, November 30th. Hello, Cyber Monday.

You'll also want to shop on December 1st or after to redeem any Yes2You rewards you earned throughout the event.

More Ways to Save at Kohl's on Black Friday

  • Take advantage of Kohl's Cash. You know how Kohl's has certain brands that are always excluded from its coupons and promotions? Well, they're not excluded from Kohl's Cash. That means you can use your rewards on any Cyber Monday deals.
  • Stack your coupons. Kohl's is the site for stacking coupons — and that goes for Black Friday, as well. From November 25th through November 29th, customers can use code THANKS15 to slash 15% off their purchases. Some brands and doorbusters are excluded from the coupon, but others aren't.
  • Download the app. If you're shopping in-store, you'll want to have the Kohl's app on your phone for instant access to all your current offers. These deals are found in the app's "Wallet" section, and it makes checkouts a breeze.
  • Sign up for Yes2You rewards. Each month, Yes2You members are awarded $5 Kohl's Cash for every $100 they spend. That can really add up if you're making some big Black Friday purchases. The program is totally free to join, and you can enroll here.
  • Don't bother with a price match. According to its website, Kohl’s is temporarily suspending its price-matching policy during the Black Friday madness.
  • Never pay for shipping. If your order doesn't hit the minimum free-shipping threshold, just opt for free in-store pickup instead.
