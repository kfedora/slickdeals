Nordstrom shoppers live for two big events each year: the store's summertime Anniversary Sale and its Black Friday extravaganza. From store hours to exclusive deals on your favorite designer brands, we're sharing our Nordstrom Black Friday review for 2020.

Nordstrom 2020 Black Friday Sale Review

When Will the Nordstrom Black Friday 2020 Ad Be Released?

  • Nordstrom is currently offering a sneak peek of 30 select Black Friday discounts online
  • The retailer will reveal the rest of its Black Friday deals on Wednesday, November 27th

While Nordstrom doesn't release an official Black Friday ad per se, it has a history of unveiling its holiday deals right before the sale goes live online. This year, the retailer is offering up a special preview of 30 select Black Friday deals to get shoppers hyped about the upcoming event. The rest of the store's deals will be revealed on Wednesday, November 27th — and we've heard there will be thousands to choose from.

In 2018, the store added a one-day-only bonus for its Nordy Club members: a $20 Nordstrom Note with any purchase of $300 or more. Keep an eye out for a similar incentive this time around.

What Time Does Nordstrom Open on Thanksgiving and Black Friday?

  • Just like Nordstrom Rack, Nordstrom is closed on Thanksgiving Day
  • Nordstrom will open its doors between 8 a.m. and 10 a.m. on Black Friday, although hours vary by location

While most stores will be open for business on Thanksgiving, Nordstrom is known for giving its employees the day off instead.

Nordstrom's Black Friday hours vary from store to store, but most locations open anywhere from 8 a.m.– 10 a.m. on Friday morning.

When Will the 2020 Nordstrom Black Friday Sale Begin Online?

  • Midnight PT on Wednesday, November 27th, 2020

Nordstrom's Black Friday deals are just as good online, so you can browse the sale at home in your PJs if you're so inclined. Plus, the store offers free shipping and free returns year-round.

This year's online sale will kick off on Wednesday night at midnight PT, and the savings will continue through Cyber Monday. Still, the store cautions that its Black Friday deals are only available in limited quantities and while supplies last, so we recommend shopping as early as possible to get what you want.

One other exciting thing about the 2020 Nordstrom Black Friday sale? New items will be added to the mix on November 29th and Cyber Monday.

Keep in mind that Nordy Club members frequently get first dibs on the store's sales, so consider that one more reason to join prior to the main event.

Which Deals Should You Focus on at Nordstrom This Black Friday?

Let's face it: There will be tons of clothing sales going on at the same time, and you're going to see lower prices on fall and winter styles elsewhere. But what sets Nordstrom apart from its competitors is its quality.

The store carries top designers, which means its Black Friday sale will include major markdowns on luxury pieces as well as on some smaller brands. You might be able to buy a pair of ankle boots for $20 less somewhere else, but you may sacrifice quality by doing so.

If you love a particular label or are saving up for a designer handbag, be sure to check the brand's official site to see what kind of price drop it's offering. After all, not everything Nordstrom carries will be on sale. For instance, Tory Burch will likely offer 30% off all purchases of $250 or more, whereas Nordstrom may just have a few Tory Burch bags and shoes included in its deals.

Also, compare prices with other department stores like Bloomingdale's, Neiman Marcus, Lord & Taylor, Macy's and Saks Fifth Avenue. Even T.J. Maxx and Stein Mart carry overlapping brands, so you may be able to snag a better deal on a certain item by shopping with them instead.

You'll find additional markdowns at Nordstrom Rack, which typically slashes an extra 30% off clearance items during its Black Friday event. Our advice? Create a list of your favorites at each store and check which retailers carry the exact items you want. Then, see how the prices compare once the deals go live.

Some of this year's deals — like the Spanx faux-leather leggings and Topshop Carly Coat — are repeats from the Nordstrom Anniversary Sale, but we're seeing a lot of fresh finds, too. Based on previous holiday deals and the store's most recent anniversary event, we anticipate the following discounts:

  • J.Crew outwear & blazers 40–60% off
  • Madewell up to 60% off boots & handbags
  • Something Navy up to 60% off apparel
  • Pajamas & loungewear up to 60% off

When is the Best Time to Shop?

  • As early as you can online starting Wednesday, November 27th at midnight PT for the best overall selection
  • During Nordstrom Note bonus sales
  • On Cyber Monday for larger purchases

Nordstrom opens its doors on Black Friday between 8 a.m. and 10 a.m. nationwide, but its online deals officially start at midnight PT on Wednesday, November 27th. For people who hate crowds, shopping online as early as possible is likely your best bet — and you'll get the best selection.

Nordstrom even has a few of its Black Friday deals available right now, like the Free People knit tunic and the Sam Edelman hooded puffer jacket.

That said, you may want to hold off until Cyber Monday if you plan on spending a considerable amount. Last year, Nordstrom gave shoppers a $50 promotional e-certificate when they spent $250 in-store or online during its Cyber Monday sale.

More Ways to Save at Nordstrom on Black Friday

  • Join the Nordy Club. Free to join, the Nordy Club is the store's rewards program. It's available to regular Nordstrom shoppers and cardholders alike, and it lets you earn rewards, called Nordstrom Notes, based on how much you spend over the course of a year.
  • Download the app. Install the Nordstrom app on your smartphone and enable push notifications to get the latest deal updates while you're on the go.
  • Double your rewards. If you're a Nordstrom cardholder, be sure to use your Personal Double Points Day(s) when you shop the sale to earn double the points on your purchases both in-store and online. You can schedule your Double Points Day whenever you choose during checkout at Nordstrom.com or via the Nordstrom app.
  • Consider the Nordstrom credit card. Speaking of cardholder perks, the store is offering new Nordstrom credit cardmembers a $60 Bonus Note now through December 2nd. Cardholders also enjoy early access to the store's Anniversary Sale plus 3 points per dollar when they shop in stores or online at Nordstrom, Nordstrom Rack, HauteLook and Trunk Club. That said, a credit card is a serious financial commitment. Make sure to read through the terms and conditions before you apply.
