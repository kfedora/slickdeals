Hide your TV in plain sight

My folks wanted to replace their old TV when they moved into a new place. They were also dead set on hiding it inside a cabinet so they wouldn't have a big, black rectangle on the wall when it wasn't in use. It turns out, a Samsung Frame TV ended up being the perfect solution.

The Frame's gallery-like bezel can be swapped out for different colors (sold separately), so it easily blends with your home's decor.

The Frame itself, well, it looks like a frame. So it easily doubles as a work of art when you're not binge-watching. Display a selection of masterworks from galleries around the world. Or perhaps even more satisfying — display a few of the countless photos you've taken with your phone and never made prints of.

Movie night can be a work of art, too

The QN43LS03T's 43" screen looks great whether you're watching a Blu-ray or 4K content from your favorite streaming service. Though you really don't need native 4K content to enjoy the picture quality. Samsung's 4K AI Upscaling enhances standard-definition and HD video signals so they look their very best on the Ultra HD display.

And thanks to Quantum Dot Technology, these QLED TVs can deliver 100% color volume in the DCI-P3 color space — that's over a billion shades of color. In simpler terms, it means that the TV can display vivid, accurate colors at any brightness level, and HDR content looks stunning. I was so impressed when I saw it in person, that I snagged a mid-sized version of last year's model as a Christmas present for my parents so they wouldn't decide to build a new cabinet to hide their ancient TV. 

Dual backlighting optimizes contrast

The backlighting on a typical LED-LCD TV uses LEDs with a fixed color temperature. This year, Samsung has upgraded The Frame with dual-LED technology. That means the TV can alternate between warm and cool LEDs depending on what best suits the content. It also reduces light leakage for improved contrast.

Smart stuff

Ready for a movie marathon? This smart TV doesn't disappoint, with loads of ways to watch your favorite content. The major players are all there, including Netflix®, Amazon Prime Video, and YouTube. It also includes the Apple TV® streaming app, so you can rent, buy, and watch titles from Apple's video library, and subscribe to Apple TV channels, all without connecting a separate device. The QN43LS03T also supports Apple AirPlay® 2, so you'll be able to stream content wirelessly from your iOS® device or Mac® to this massive screen. Android users can mirror content using the SmartThings app.

Samsung's slim OneRemote is one of the smartest remotes we've seen. It automatically detects, identifies, and controls your connected devices and content. It also has a built-in voice assistant which you can activate by pressing the mic button or by saying “Hi Bixby."

Already have a voice assistant you love? This TV is also compatible with Amazon Alexa and Google Home devices.

Art Mode transforms The Frame into a beautiful work of art when you're not watching TV.

A rotating digital gallery

The Frame includes a curated collection of 20 pre-loaded pieces of art. But if you crave more options, you can purchase individual pieces from the Art Store or pay a monthly membership fee for unlimited access to the whole catalog. And if you subscribe, The Frame will learn your taste and automatically curate your collection accordingly.

Worried about power consumption? Don't be — The Frame has a built-in motion sensor, so it will only display art when there is someone in the room. You can adjust the motion sensor timer so the screen turns off when it no longer detects motion. There are also built-in ambient light sensors that adjust the image to your room's lighting conditions.

Display your own great works, too

It's easy to upload and display your own photographs or digital versions of other kinds of art, too. Load them up on a USB flash drive, or simply use the Samsung SmartThings app on your phone. You can use the app's filters to edit and improve your uploaded images. Further customize the artwork display by choosing from different matte color and layout options.

Super-streamlined

The QN43LS03T keeps a very low profile when hung with Samsung's included No Gap Wall Mount. It allows the Frame TV to rest nearly flush against the wall, just like a real picture frame. And you don't have to clutter up the view with a bunch of wires thanks to the included One Connect Box. You hook up all your gear — Blu-ray player, cable box, game console, etc. — to the hub, then run a single slim cable up to the TV to supply it with signals and power.

Personalize it

And if you want The Frame to blend in or stand out in your decor, customize it with an add-on bezel (sold separately) to match or contrast the rest of your art or furnishings.
